Title
PsA compared to psoriasis - best paper ever


Introduction:
PsA is a chronic autoimmune disease with prevalence of 14%.
Psoriasis is a skin disorder which is connected with PsA.

Methodology:
Logistic regression was used to compare disease status with possible lifestyle factors.

Results:
931 patients with PsA and 4,441 patients with psoriasis participanted in the UK Biobank
